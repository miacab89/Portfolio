About Me

Mia was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas, and is now currently a new resident in Austin. Her background as an artist and musician has led her to this rewarding path of Software Programming. Her last educational endeavor in Philosophy/Law gave her the tools to think outside the traditonal framework. As a programmer, she intends on using this same approach for her career by creating resources for human development. She have one cat (Scarlett), currently in three active bands, and occassionally works on illustration commissions for album art and logos.
